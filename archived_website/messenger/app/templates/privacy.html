Open Source Commitment

We believe in open development and transparency. All source code is public and our security practices are open for review.

  • Public Code: Available on GitHub.
  • Auditable Security: Implementations can be reviewed by anyone.
  • Community Driven: Contributions and forks are encouraged.
  • Transparency: We collect only the data that is necessary.

Data Handling & Retention

We collect only the data needed for operating and improving our services, which includes:

  • Data Collection: IP addresses, browser details, access timestamps, and user submissions.
  • Data Usage: For functionality, performance monitoring, security, and improvements.

We also minimize data retention:

  • Converted Files: Deleted after download or session end.
  • Chat Messages: Exist only for the duration of your session.
  • AI Conversations: Not stored after your session ends.
